# Coupon Acceptance Analysis

A brief exploration of the factors that influence whether a driver accepts 
a coupon delivered to their cell phone while driving.

## Summary of Findings
- Bar coupons are most accepted by regular bar-goers without kids, particularly under 30
- Coffee house coupons peak at 81% acceptance among regular visitors receiving offers at 10AM
